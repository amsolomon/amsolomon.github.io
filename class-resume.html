University of Florida

  • College of Agricultural and Life Sciences
  • Food and Resource Economics Department
  • Food and Agribusiness Marketing and Management Specialization

    • The specialization is for students interested in food and fiber systems management, marketing, finance and international business and employment opportunities and sales and managerial positions in agribusiness firm.

    Summary of the courses taken at the University of Florida:

    AEB3103 Principles of Food and Resource Economics - Fall 2008

  • Principles of economics as applied to agriculture; economic problems of the agricultural industry and the individual farmer.

    • AEB3510 Quantitative Methods in Food and Resource Economic - Fall 2008

  • Develop the understanding of finite mathematical tools used in economics and business decision making.

    • AEB3133 Principles of Agribusiness Management - Fall 2008

  • Management principles, application of economic principles, budgeting techniques, and principles of management strategy.

    • AEB2451 Valuing Environmental Protection in Florida - Fall 2008

  • How economists value the environment and regulations designed to protect our natural resources from overuse and degradation.

    • AEB3300 Agricultural and Food Marketing - Spring 2009

  • Examination of agricultural and food marketing from a system and individual firm perspective.

    • AEB3122 Financial Planning for Agribusiness - Spring 2009

  • Introduction to basic financial and managerial decision-making for small agribusinesses.

    • AEB3550 Agricultural Data Analysis in Food and Resource Economics - Spring 2009

  • Analysis of agricultural data. Incorporates statistical and economic theory into the analysis of agricultural problems.

    • GEB3113 Principles of Entrepreneurship - Spring 2009

  • Practical, hands-on understanding of the stages of entrepreneurial process.

    • AEB3144 Introduction to Agricultural Finance - Summer 2009

  • Basic financial statements, investment frameworks, and theories of financial intermediation.

    • AEB3281 Agricultural Macroeconomics - Summer 2009

  • An introduction to the world economy from a macroeconomic perspective.

    • AEB4283 International Development Policy - Summer 2009

  • How factors such as poverty, population, technology, resources, trade and the environment affect man's effort to develop.

    • AEB4334 Agricultural Price Analysis and Consumer Behavior - Fall 2009

  • Quantitative measurement of factors affecting agricultural and resource prices; seasonal and cyclical fluctuations.

    • AEB4342 Agribusiness and Food Marketing Management - Fall 2009

  • Application of marketing and management principles to agribusiness and food marketing problems faced by managers.

    • AEB4424 Human Resources Management in Agribusiness - Fall 2009

  • Strategic and effective management of human resources. Human resource management concepts and techniques.

    • AEB4309 Food Wholesaling and Retail Marketing - Fall 2009

  • Students are introduced to food industry trends, issues and opportunities that exist both in the U.S. and the world.
